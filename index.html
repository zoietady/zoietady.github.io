Waste Generation and Economic Output

Choropleth Bubble Plot References

This project is created by Euan Giligan and Zoie Tad-y in partial completion of the requirements in the course COS30045-Data Visualisation of Swinburne University of Technology. This aims to provide viewers with a glance of the ever growing problem that is polution.

This project utilizes that dataset provided by The World Bank Data Catalog entitled "What a Waste".

This is mainly comprised of two charts, a choropleth that focuses on location and individual varaibles, and a bubble plot that is aimed to show the relationship between variables. The Choropleth is meant to show the scale of each country in terms of population, econmic output, and generating waste, while the Bubble Plot seeks to depict more clearly the realtionship between each contires economic output, population, and waste output.
